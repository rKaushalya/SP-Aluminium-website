About Us

SP Aluminium & Glass Work has been a leading supplier of premium aluminum products and expert glass solutions for over 10 years. We pride ourselves on precision, quality, and exceptional client service.

Founded on the principles of innovation and excellence, we specialize in custom aluminum fabrication and sophisticated glass installations. Our team of skilled artisans and engineers works hand-in-hand with clients to deliver powerful, beautiful, and lasting solutions.

Over the years, we've expanded our expertise and now proudly offer a comprehensive range of products, designed and developed to solve everyday challenges with elegance and efficiency. Our journey is built on the core values of integrity, collaboration, and delivering exceptional value at every step. We look forward to partnering with you on your next project.

Our Main Services

Aluminium Fabrication

Expert crafting of custom aluminium structures, frames, and profiles. We handle all phases, from precision cutting to final installation, ensuring structural integrity and modern aesthetics.

Custom Glass Work

High-quality glass solutions, including partitions, safety glass installations, shower cubicles, and shopfronts. Enhancing spaces with clarity, light, and modern design.

Doors & Windows

Design and installation of modern sliding doors, fixed windows, casement windows, and bi-fold doors, offering superior durability, thermal efficiency, and security.

Curtain Walls & Cladding

Professional installation of aluminium curtain walls and exterior cladding for commercial and residential projects, providing structural strength and a high-end finish.

Aluminium Pantry Units

Waterproof and termite-resistant aluminium kitchen and pantry cupboards, custom-designed for modern homes, combining function and style.

Design & Consultancy

Full project consultancy and bespoke design services to develop unique aluminium and glass solutions for challenging or custom architectural requirements.
